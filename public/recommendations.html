Sun Safety Recommendations

Personalized recommendations based on UV index and skin type

For Fair Skin

Use SPF 50+ sunscreen, wear protective clothing, and seek shade during peak hours.

For Medium Skin

Use SPF 30+ sunscreen, wear a hat, and limit sun exposure between 10 AM and 4 PM.

For Dark Skin

Use SPF 15+ sunscreen, wear sunglasses, and stay hydrated while outdoors.
